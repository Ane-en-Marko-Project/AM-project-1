How to play:

Builder-defender is a game that is all about creating buildings, defending against enemies and having fun. To place a building, click on the type of building that you would like to place (in the bottom left corner) and click on the location that you want to place it in. To repair a building you need 50 pieces of gold.Click on the building that you would like to repair and it's health will be restored

Building types:

Wood-harvester: generates 2 extra wood logs per second
Stone-harvester: generates 2 extra rocks per second
Gold-mine: generates 2 extra gold-pieces per second
Tower: shoots 2 arrows at enemies per second

Resource types:

Wood: used to create buildings
Stone: used to create buildings
Gold: used to repair buildings
